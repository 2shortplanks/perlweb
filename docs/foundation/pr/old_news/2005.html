January 24, 2005 - YAPC::NA 2005 Call For Papers

YAPC::NA 2005 (Yet Another Perl Conference, North America) has just released its call-for-papers. The close of the call-for-papers is April 18, 2005 at 11:59 pm. Questions regarding submissions, please email na-author@yapc.org.

We would love to hear from potential sponsors. Please contact the organizers at na-sponsor@yapc.org to learn about the benefits of sponsorship.

Other information regarding the conference (e.g. venue, registration specifics) will be announced soon. We look forward to your submissions and a great conference!
