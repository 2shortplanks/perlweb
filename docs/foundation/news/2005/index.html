March 1, 2005
Grant Given to Ivan Tubert-Brohman for AnnoCPAN

The Perl Foundation is proud to announce the latest grant aimed at furthering development in Perl, Ivan Tubert-Brohman will receive $1000 as a development grant to implement AnnoCPAN, a web interface for the documentation of all the modules on CPAN, where users can add annotations on the margin of specific paragraphs throughout the POD.

January 24, 2005
YAPC::NA::2005 Call For Papers

YAPC::NA 2005 (Yet Another Perl Conference, North America) has just released its call-for-papers. The close of the call-for-papers is April 18, 2005 at 11:59 pm. Questions regarding submissions, please email na-author@yapc.org.

We would love to hear from potential sponsors. Please contact the organizers at na-sponsor@yapc.org to learn about the benefits of sponsorship.
