[% page.banner = "October 25, 2001 - YAPC::Europe 2002 to be in Munich, Germany" %] [% page.title = page.banner %]

The third European YAPC for 2002 is being held in Munich, Southern Germany, and is being hosted by the Munich Perl Mongers. The conference will be held from September 18-20, 2002. Check back here, or at the YAPC::Europe Home Page for updates of location, CFP, and schedules.
