[% page.title ="Who's Who in Perl 6, parrot, & Pugs" %] Architecture Team
  • Larry Wall is the inventor of Perl, rn, patch, and many other wonderful things. He is the language designer for Perl 6.
  • Damian Conway is helping Larry Wall with the design of Perl 6, providing a different perspective from most. He also writes the Exegeses, which explain by example Larry's design documents (the Apocalypses).
  • Chip Salzenberg is the current Parrot internals designer.
  • Leopold Tötsch is the patch monster and the current Parrot pumpking.
  • chromatic is the project secretary. He is at least partially responsible for delaying Apocalypse 12 with that Roles idea but lays no particular claim to finding odd bugs.
  • Allison Randal is the project manager. Her job is to herd cats, which might explain why she hacks on the Perl 6 compiler.
  • Hugo van der Sanden, knows more about Perl 5 internals than any one person should, which helps to make better decisions for Perl 6.
  • Luke Palmer is the Perl 6 language list monitor and co-author of the retroactive Synopses.
  • Nathan Torkington is the project manager emeritus.
  • Dan Sugalski used to be the internals chief for Perl 6. His job was to coordinate the design of the parser, compiler, optimizer, and runtime engine.
Parrot Code Manager / Pumpking
  • Leopold Toetsch is currently the leader of the Parrot development team.
  • Steve Fink was the code chief for Parrot 0.0.9 to 0.0.12.
  • Jeff Goff was the code chief for Parrot 0.0.5 to 0.0.8.
  • Simon Cozens was the the code chief for Parrot 0.0.1 to 0.0.4.
Resident Journalists
  • Matt Fowles is the current Perl 6 Summarizer, and regularly churns out the List Summaries.
  • Piers Cawley wrote "This Week on Perl 6" from June 2002 through October 2004.
  • Bryan Warnock used to compile the Perl 6 Digest with Simon Cozens.
