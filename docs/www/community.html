Perl's great strength lies within the active community of Perl developers and users.

One of Perls biggest strengths is it's community...

Local Communities

  • Perl Mongers - find your local perl group from over [% perl_stats.monger_groups %] groups world wide.
  • Or go directly to: French Perl Mongers, Italian Perl Mongers, German Perl Mongers websites

Community Web Sites

  • PerlMonks - A community committed to sharing Perl knowledge and coding tips. The site contains questions and answers, useful snippets, and a library of code.
  • perl.org - your current location
  • Planet Perl - an aggregation of selected perl journals
  • Perlsphere - another aggregation of selected perl blogs
  • use Perl; - read community news and personal journals, or start your own journal

Mail Lists

lists.perl.org has information on all the Perl mail lists you can join. Here are a few of note:

  • perl-beginners - need help? try here!
  • beginners-cgi - cgi specific help
  • BioPerl - bioinformatics
  • Win32 Perl - for windows Perl users
