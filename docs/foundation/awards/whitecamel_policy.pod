=head1 White Camel Awards Selection Policies

=over

=item 1

The award is granted for non-technical contributions to Perl.

=item 1

Prior recipients of the White Camel award aren't eligible to receive
it a second time.

=item 1

Active TPF volunteers are not eligible to receive the award. This includes
board members, steering committee members, grants committee members, and
members of any subcommittees.

=item 1

Current and prior development grant recipients are eligible for the award.

=item 1

The White Camel awards committee is made up of award recipients from
previous years.

=item 1

The vote of the awards committee is final. The board has veto power
over the committee's decisions, but won't use that power unless it's
absolutely necessary (which means probably never).

=back
