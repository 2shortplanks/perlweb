European YAPC's and National Workshops now have a foundation.

The main goal of the newly formed YAPC Europe Foundation is to provide assistance on request to any European crew setting up a conference (be it a YAPC::Europe or a national workshop).

The foundation can bring help in terms of experience from previous organisers, resources: web space, CVS, mailing lists... (online payment will also be offered), etc.

Of course, its chief concern is to make sure there is a yearly YAPC::Europe, and while the Belfast Perl mongers are organizing the YAPC::Europe::2004 conference in Northern Ireland with enthusiasm, YEF is already looking for next years candidates.

Any dedicated crew interested in organizing the YAPC::Europe conference in 2005 should send an informal application, stating their interest, to:

"YAPC::Europe conference proposal" committee@yapceurope.org

Questions about the application and help to present it can also be asked at this same address.

Deadline for applications: August 15th 2004

For general information about the venue requirements, check the YAPC::Venue module on CPAN, and look at the previous venues at:

http://www.yapceurope.org

Norbert Gruener (President)
for YAPC Europe Foundation (YEF)
