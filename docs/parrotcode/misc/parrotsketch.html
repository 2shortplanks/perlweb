[% page.title = "Parrot Sketch" %]

Each week, a group of developers working on Parrot and Parrot-related projects gathers online to discuss progress, changes and the state of the metaphorical weather. This meeting currently occurs each Tuesday at 18:30 UTC, in the #parrotsketch channel on irc (irc.perl.org).

Participating in the meeting is by invitation only, however lurkers are welcome, and non-invitees may send private messages to the participants with questions to be asked or answered. The full logs of each meeting are 100% public.
