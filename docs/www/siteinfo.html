  • About the website
    Designed and implemented between OSCON 2002 and OSCON 2003, the new perl.org is designed to be the central directory of all things perl. The team responsible for the update is Lisa Wolfisch, Robert Spier, and Ask Bjoern Hansen. The site is relatively browser independent and aims to comply with major accessibility standards. Our center of operations is The Perl NOC. You can track our progress at the NOC Log.

    When you need to link to Perl, we want you to think perl.org
