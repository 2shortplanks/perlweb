The Perl Conference

  • An annual event since 1997.
  • Now part of the O'Reilly Open Source Convention.
  • The largest annual gathering of Perl users and developers.
  • Tutorials, technical presentations, social events, and more.
  • Access images, audio and video from the Perl Conference.

Yet Another Perl Conference (YAPC)

  • A series of popular annual conferences with roots in the Perl user community.
  • Annual conferences in North America and and Europe (and other continents, see the YAPC site for more).
  • An inexpensive alternative to other conferences with prohibitive registration fees.
  • Registration fees at YAPC average less than $100.
  • A friendly venue for sharing research, learning new techniques, and talking with leading developers.

The German Perl Workshop

  • Brings together Perl module developers and advanced Perl users in the German-speaking Perl community.
  • Also draws participants from the Perl community at large.
  • Like YAPC, this is a grassroots effort with minimal registration fees.

Local Perl Mongers meetings

  • Over 200 local Perl Monger groups spread over six continents.
  • Technical and social meetings.
  • Organizational support from The Perl Foundation.
  • Find a group near you!
