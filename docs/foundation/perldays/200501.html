How to get there

The meeting will be held at the O'Reilly campus in Sebastopol, CA.

O'Reilly Media, Inc.

Oakland (OAK) and San Francisco (SFO) airports are roughly the same distance from Sebastopol. If you don't encounter traffic, the drive takes about an hour and a half. Oakland's smaller and easier to deal with, but more flights come into SFO.

Driving Directions

From San Francisco:
  1. Take US-101 North out of the city.
  2. Continue along US-101 (approx. 55 miles) until the Hwy. 116 West exit towards Sebastopol. (Don't be fooled by the earlier Hwy. 116 East exit.)
  3. Turn left at the light (at the bottom of the exit ramp).
  4. Continue to follow 116 for approx. 8 miles.
  5. Now you're in downtown Sebastopol. 116 winds through downtown, taking you past a small shopping center on the right that's home to Whole Foods. Be sure to turn right at the next light, which keeps you on Hwy. 116.
  6. Follow 116 out of town for approx. 1 mile.
  7. Turn right into the large parking lot with a light gray campus beyond. It's across the street from Taco Bell.
  8. Welcome to O'Reilly Media.
  9. The Tarsier conference room is a semi-detached glass-walled room behind Building C, the building farthest to the right as you first turn into the parking lot.

Public Transportation

Airport Express buses from San Francisco and Oakland airports to Santa Rosa run about every hour to two hours. You'll need to take a cab (~$25) from Santa Rosa to Sebastopol.
